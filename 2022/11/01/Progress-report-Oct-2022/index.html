Routine

There’s something that I want to share here that I’ve worked on a quit some time that is my dayli routine.
This isn’t acctualy a thing that worked for me, maybe will not work for you but can help you in some way. I’ve
tried a lot of things to have a better day in terms of productivity, criativity and focusing but the folowing
things is what worked for me:

  1. Wake up and go take a bath (7am)
  2. Go to my office (inside my home)
  3. Do a coffee
  4. Seat in front of my notebook and start the day solving some puzzle (math things or just logic questions,
    mostly envolving math)
  5. Solve some web security puzzle or just a challenge
  6. Read 1 article
  7. Then start working
  8. I stop in the main job and start doing my researches mostly at 5pm

This have worked for me and I don’t have perspective to change this for a while but I’ve sure that will change in
some way in the next months, because we all try to improve the things however, to improve we are submited to
brake the routine as well (for now I don’t wan’t to broke this routine).

I’ve doing some interesting things that I don’t know if it’s a good thing but are working, I’m putting some
time pressure in every work/study that I make so I avoid procrastination.
